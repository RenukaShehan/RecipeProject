Kos Ata Kalupol Maluwa Recipe

Kos Ata Kalupol Maluwa, a delicious Sri Lankan jackfruit seed curry.

Ingredients:

  • 1 TBSP Coriander Seeds
  • 3/4 TBSP Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 TBSP Black Peppercorns
  • 1/4 TSP Mustard Seeds
  • 1 Clove
  • 1 Green Cardamom
  • 1/4 TSP Fennel Seeds
  • Curry Leaves, 6 + More
  • 1/2 TSP Fenugreek
  • 25 Cempedak / Jackfruit Seeds
  • Pandan Leaves, 3 + 2
  • Pinch Sea Salt
  • 2 TBSP Coconut Butter
  • 1 TBSP Desiccated Coconut
  • 2 Red Onion Finely Minced
  • Pinch Demerara Sugar
  • 3 Cloves Garlic Finely Minced
  • 1/2 TSP Turmeric Powder
  • Kashmiri Chili Powder, 1/2 TSP Adjust To Preference
  • 2 TBSP Basmati Rice
  • 250 g Coconut Milk
  • 260 g Coconut Cream
  • 2 Green Chili Coarsely Sliced, Deseeded

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, toast coriander, cumin, pepper, mustard, clove, cardamom, fennel, curry leaves, and fenugreek until aromatic. Grind into a powder and set aside.
  2. Boil Cempedak Seeds: Wash the cempedak or jackfruit seeds. In a saucepot, add seeds, 3 pandan leaves, and a pinch of salt. Boil for 15-20 mins until husk can be peeled easily. Peel and discard husk.
  3. Toast Coconut and Onions: In a skillet, melt coconut butter. Add desiccated coconut, then onion. Season with salt and sugar. Sauté until lightly caramelized.
  4. Add Aromatics: Stir in garlic, turmeric, chili, basmati rice, curry leaves, and the spice blend. Sauté until well combined and aromatic.
  5. Cook with Coconut: Pour in coconut milk and cream. Add cempedak seeds and green chilies. Stir and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 15-20 mins until oil separates from milk solids.
  6. Final Touch: Submerge 2 pandan leaves, bring to a simmer, and cook until the liquid evaporates. Stir occasionally to prevent burning. Adjust seasoning with salt, remove pandan leaves, and serve immediately.
